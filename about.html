Zain Karim

(he/him)

b. 2000, Texas, USA

Zain Karim is a student and photographer with a passion for problem-solving, fostering creativity through software, and exploring the intersection of art and technology. Currently studying computer science at the University of Texas at Dallas, his interests focus on computer vision, image processing algorithms, and computational photography.

Zain's work spans creative coding projects and visual storytelling, aiming to bridge technical innovation with artistic expression. Whether designing software that interacts with digital images or capturing evocative moments through his lens, he seeks to challenge conventional boundaries between technology and art.

When he's not at his computer, Zain enjoys cooking pasta, making photographs, and listening to jazz. He believes in blending creative inspiration with technical expertise to build solutions that are both innovative and human-centered.
